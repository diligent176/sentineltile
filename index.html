Services:

We provide a complete tile service, or any part of the process. From visualizing and designing your project, to removing waste from the jobsite, to grout cleaning and restoration. We've chosen to specialize in high-end custom installations, but also deliver commercial work from sterile bathrooms to elegant spas.

  • Design the entire project
  • Remove existing tile or substrate
  • Locate, deliver & install tile or stone (interior & exterior)
  • Install custom tile insets, borders and edge detail
  • Install backer board, sub-floor, cultured stone, precast concrete, waterproofing & crack isolation membranes
  • Concrete countertops, fireplace surrounds, precast terrazzo, stone tile, cladding
  • Grout cleaning, recolouring and sealing, silicon repair and installation
